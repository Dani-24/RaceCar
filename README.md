### Controls

- A/S/D/W: Movement
- Shift: Turbo
- R: Reset

### Debug

- F1: Debug mode
	- Show Colliders & Hitboxes
	- Space: Jump
- F2: Free Camera movement mode
	- Left/Right: Camera Horizontal movement
	- Up/Down: Camera Zoom in/zoom out
	- Mouse Right Click & Drag: Camera Angle
	- M/N Camera Y axis
